myfeeder Keyboard Shortcuts

Quick reference cheatsheet

Article Navigation

j / kNext / previous article
EnterOpen article in reading pane
oOpen original URL in new tab

Feed Navigation

n / pNext / previous feed
rRefresh current feed
TabCycle panel focus (feeds → articles → reading pane)

Go To (g-chords)

g then aGo to All Articles
g then sGo to Starred
g then bGo to Boards

Article Actions

mToggle read / unread
sToggle star
bAdd to board
vSave to Raindrop
Shift+AMark all read in current feed

General

/Focus search
EscapeClear selection / close dialogs
?Show keyboard shortcuts overlay
